FAQ's

Find answers to your frequently asked questions below! We'll keep this up-to-date, and if you can't find an answer to your question - just get in touch with us at robotwars@mentorn.tv

  • Can I enter my robot, and how do I do this?

    Anyone can apply to enter a robot for Robot Wars. As soon as we have confirmation of a new series of Robot Wars all the information required will be posted on the official sites.

    Facebook: www.facebook.com/UKRobotWars

    Twitter: https://twitter.com/UKRobotWars

  • What's the age limit and how many team members can we have?

    The minimum age for a member of a team is 7 years old. They must also be accompanied at all times by a parent or guardian and adhere to the strict rules around filming hours per day. Each team can have no more than 4 members.

  • Where can I get tickets for the Live Shows?

    Live Show audience tickets for the new series of Robot Wars are now available from the following website:

    www.lostintv.com

     

  • Is there anyone to help me build a robot?

    We are very keen to encourage as many people as possible to get involved with engineering and the show. However building a combat robot is a complex and dangerous undertaking, robot wars cannot offer assistance in building your robot and any build is undertaken at your own risk.

    We advise that teams have safety as their first priority, be exceptionally careful and take adequate safety precautions at every stage of the build. If it is your first time building a robot it is advisable to discuss your plans and building process with the wider robot building community.
