IRAN FIGHTER

robotic competitions

General information

The RoboSkill competition occurs twice a year in Kerman-Iran.

This competition is broadcasted from official IR-Iran's TV and is held by professional and technical organization of Kerman , Lavan robotic institute and Shahid Mahdavi school.

RoboSkill competition has most professional and biggest fight arena in Iran (12x12 meters) which includes hidden weapons.

The competition is held in three 30kg , 60kg and 120kg leagues.

Arena information

The Robo Skill competition arena has the following specifications :

Dimensions of the land: 12x12 meters

Height above ground: 50 cm

Ground floor: Flat iron sheet

material of internal walls: iron

Height of internal walls: 30 cm

Ground racing is 100 cm away from the outer guard.

The height of the outer walls of the earth: 3 meters

The gender of external walls: a combination of iron and polycarbonate 6mm sheet

How do you win Robot Wars?

In each round of the competition the Robots will engage in a timed battle, with the winners decided by either ‘knockout’ or, if the time runs out with no clear winner, a ‘judges' decision’ made by our panel of three expert judges.
